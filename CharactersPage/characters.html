Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson (born May 12,1956) is the main protagonist of the Simpsons series or show. He is the spouse of Marge Simpson and a father of Bart, Lisa, and Maggie Simpson. Homer is overweight, weighing 239 pounds, he is defined as a lazy character and often ignorant to the world around him. Although Homer has many flaws, he has shown to have a great caring love, and even bravery to those he cares about and sometimes, even others he doesn’t. He also serves as the main protagonist of “The Simpsons Movie”. Homer Simpson is in his late 30s and early 40s.
