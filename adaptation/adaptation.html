The Simpson Film

The Simpsons Movie is a 2007 American animated comedy film based on the television series The Simpsons. It was directed by David Silverman and written by a team of writers from the show, including James L. Brooks, Matt Groening, Al Jean, Ian Maxtone-Graham, George Meyer, David Mirkin, Mike Scully, and Matt Selman. The film features the voices of the regular cast of the television series, including Dan Castellaneta, Julie Kavner, Nancy Cartwright, Yeardley Smith, Hank Azaria, and Harry Shearer.
